Our Background

FAVOURCOMP ENTERPRISE was formed in 2012 as a subsidiary of FAVOURCOMP ENTERPRISE and incorporated in 2017 by renowned IT professionals in Kenya. Our primary objective is to provide computer products and other services to organizations and companies in the efficient application of Information Technology.

FAVOURCOMP ENTERPRISE activities comprise the supply, design and installation of all types of computer systems. We employ qualified and innovative individuals who have the necessary skills and years of experience to respond to today's challenging IT needs.

Mission Statement

To help Customers build a reliable, secure, manageable and flexible IT Infrastructure that establishes a foundation for Clients to meet their business objectives.
