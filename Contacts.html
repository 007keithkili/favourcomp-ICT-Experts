Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and support

What is your typical response time for support requests?

+

We typically respond to support requests within 2-4 business hours during regular working hours. Emergency support requests from premium clients receive immediate attention 24/7.

Do you offer remote IT support services?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive remote IT support for most software and network-related issues. We use secure remote access tools to diagnose and fix problems without needing to be physically on-site.

What areas in Kenya do you serve?

+

We serve clients across Kenya with our main offices in Eldoret, Nairobi, and Kisumu. We also have mobile teams that can travel to other regions for on-site installations and support.

Do you provide IT equipment leasing options?

+

Yes, we offer flexible IT equipment leasing options for businesses that prefer not to make large capital investments. Our leasing plans include maintenance and support services.

How long does a typical web development project take?

+

The timeline varies based on project complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, medium complexity projects take 4-8 weeks, and complex enterprise solutions can take 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.
